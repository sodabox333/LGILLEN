Key Issues:

ENVIRONMENT

Laura Gillen is an outspoken defender of the environment:

  • She will support investments in park infrastructure projects
  • She will support and promote legislation that protects New York State's water supply
  • She will fight to keep corporations from polluting Long Island's drinking water, so clean water is freely ready for residents of Nassau County and all New Yorkers

REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH

Laura Gillen believes in reproductive justice for all people:

  • She will stand up against anti-abortion extremism, and push to reinstate Roe v. Wade
  • She will pass the Women's Health Protection Act and codify into law the right to contraception and IVF

MEDICARE AND SOCIAL SECURITY

Laura Gillen believes all people should have resources to thrive for the entirety of their lives:

  • She will fight for expanding Medicare, so all New Yorkers can access physical medicine and mental health care
  • She believes in a retirement in which people can afford their basic necessities
  • She will advocate for programs that support seniors

GUN VIOLENCE

Gillen will protect everyday people and stand up to the extreme right gun lobby:

  • She supports gun safety measures and universal background checks
  • She has worked pro-bono as a lawyer for a variety of causes including gun safety
  • She plans to support legislation that allows for safe gun storage, and she supports common-sense public safety measures
